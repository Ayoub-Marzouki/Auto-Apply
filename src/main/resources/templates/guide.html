How to Convert Your CV to PDF

From Microsoft Word:

  1. Open your CV in Microsoft Word
  2. Click File → Save As
  3. Choose PDF from the file type dropdown
  4. Click Save

From Google Docs:

  1. Open your CV in Google Docs
  2. Click File → Download → PDF Document (.pdf)
  3. The PDF will be saved to your downloads folder

From Other Formats:

Use free online converters like iLovePDF or SmallPDF to convert any document format to PDF.

How to Make a Proper CV

Essential Information:

  • Contact Details: Full name, email, phone number
  • Professional Links: LinkedIn profile, GitHub (for tech roles), portfolio website
  • Work Experience: Job titles, companies, dates, and key responsibilities
  • Education: Degrees, institutions, graduation dates
  • Skills: Technical skills, tools, languages, frameworks

Best Practices:

  • Keep it to 1-2 pages maximum
  • Use clear section headings
  • List experience in reverse chronological order
  • Use bullet points for easy scanning
  • Quantify achievements with numbers and metrics where possible
  • Tailor your CV to the specific role you're applying for
  • Proofread for spelling and grammar errors

Common Mistakes to Avoid:

  • Including personal photos (unless specifically requested)
  • Using overly creative fonts or colors that reduce readability
  • Writing long paragraphs instead of concise bullet points
  • Including irrelevant work experience
  • Lying or exaggerating about skills or experience
